Sponsorship Packages

Sponsorship packages may vary by venue location and circumstances. And sessions, tutorials and bag inserts provided by sponsors should be served after consultation with organizers.

  • Keystone / 1 slot

    • Sponsorship Fee 20,000,000 KRW
    • 20 Conference Passes
    • 5 Booths
    • 1 OST room with sponsor name (for 100 persons)
    • 2 Sponsor Session (provided by sponsor, optional)
    • Logo on attendee pass
    • Logo on attendee bag
    • 2 Full pages on Program Guidebook
    • Recognition on WebSite, Hanging banner and Standing banner

  • Diamond / 4 slots

    • Sponsorship Fee 10,000,000 KRW
    • 15 Conference Passes
    • 3 Booths
    • 1 OST room with sponsor name (for 24 persons)
    • 1 Sponsor Session (provided by sponsor, optional)
    • Logo on attendee bag
    • 1 Full pages on Program Guidebook
    • Recognition on WebSite, Hanging banner and Standing banner

  • Platinum / 6 slots

    • Sponsorship Fee 7,500,000 KRW
    • 10 Conference Passes
    • 2 Booths
    • 1 OST room with sponsor name (for 12 persons)
    • 1 Full pages on Program Guidebook
    • Recognition on WebSite and Standing banner

  • Gold / 15 slots

    • Sponsorship Fee 5,000,000 KRW
    • 7 Conference Passes
    • 1 Booths
    • 1/2 pages on Program Guidebook
    • Recognition on WebSite and Standing banner

  • Startup / 5 slots

    • Sponsorship Fee 2,500,000 KRW

    • Recognition on WebSite and Standing banner

  • Silver / unlimited

    • Recognition on WebSite and Standing banner
